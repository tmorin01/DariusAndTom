Our Story

Meeting During Lockdown

Deep into the COVID lockdown, Darius and Tom had their first date over FaceTime. To make things fun, they decided on a theme for the snowy February day: sweater weather with spiked hot cocoa - but Tom forgot to wear a sweater. A wardrobe change, and several refills later, they realized they'd been talking for over five hours and decided to plan a second date. A few days later, Tom found $20 half-burried in the snow and decided on their next date - a walk around Harvard Square - they find a fun way to spend the $20. They ended up spending $19 on two books from the Raven Used Books store that used to be on Church St. Tom bought Darius a book on the goddess Venus. Darius bought Tom a book on consciousness. They ended their date with a stop at the Wilks Footbridge where took off their masks and shared their first kiss.

18 Feb 21

Dating Through Grad School

Dating during COVID required a little cerativity - picnics in the park, watching Netflix together over zoom, and outdoor dining in the snow. As the world re-opened, Darius and Tom finally got to meet each others friends and families. They spent a weekend in Ogunquit, ME, went on their first white water rafting trip, attended their first post-COVID concert, and in the spring of 2022, they graduated from BU!

1 Sep 22

Moving to Mass. Ave.

In September, 2022, Darius and Tom moved in together to an apartment on Mass. Ave. in Cambridge. It's been a fun couple of years curating their decor, cooking dinners together, hosting their annual Pride Brunch.

Aug 24

Getting Engaged

Last year in August, on a trip to Provincetown, Darius and Tom decided to buy engagement rings from a jewelry store called Exuma on Commercial St. Later in November, when the rings came in the mail, Tom and Darius went on a walk, back to the Wilks Footbridge where they went on their first in-person date and asked each other to get married. We're so excited to celebrate with our closest friends and family in May of 2026!
